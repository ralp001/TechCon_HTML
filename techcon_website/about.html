Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts in a university lecture hall. Driven by a shared passion for innovation and knowledge exchange, it quickly grew into an annual event, drawing attendees and speakers from across the nation. Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of discussing emerging technologies, from the rise of mobile computing to the advent of AI.

In 2015, TechCon expanded internationally, hosting its first global speakers and introducing interactive workshops that became a hallmark of the conference experience. Each year builds on the last, fostering a vibrant community dedicated to pushing technological boundaries.

Our Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to inspire, educate, and connect the global tech community. We aim to provide a platform for sharing groundbreaking ideas, fostering collaborative innovation, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders. We believe in the power of technology to transform lives and are committed to creating an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives can thrive.

Through thought-provoking keynotes, hands-on sessions, and unparalleled networking opportunities, TechCon strives to be the catalyst for technological progress and personal growth.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has been graced by some of the most influential minds in the tech world. Here are a few:

Dr. Anya Sharma - AI Pioneer

Dr. Sharma, a leading expert in artificial intelligence ethics, delivered a captivating keynote in 2019 on "AI and Society: Building a Responsible Future." Her work has significantly influenced policies on AI development globally.

Mr. Ben Carter - Cybersecurity Innovator

In 2021, Mr. Carter shared his insights on "Securing the Digital Frontier," presenting cutting-edge solutions for protecting data in an increasingly connected world. He is the CEO of SecureNet Solutions.

Ms. Chloe Zhang - Cloud Computing Visionary

Ms. Zhang, renowned for her contributions to scalable cloud architectures, spoke at TechCon 2023 about "The Infinite Scalability of Cloud: Beyond Limits." Her insights helped many organizations optimize their cloud infrastructure.
